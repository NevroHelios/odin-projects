Pizza Recipe

Description: A classic pizza recipe with your favorite toppings and a perfect crust.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and roll out the pizza dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the dough and add your favorite toppings.
  3. Sprinkle cheese on top and bake until golden and bubbly.
