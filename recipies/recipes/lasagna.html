Lasagna Recipe

Description: A delicious and hearty lasagna recipe that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan.
  3. Layer the lasagna noodles, ground beef, and ricotta cheese in a baking dish.
